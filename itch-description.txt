The Tales of JV Pirates

A pirate-themed 2D adventure game built with Python and Pygame during CraftQuest at American Center Colombo (AJAX program).

Team Typhoons shipped this as our first team game — level design, combat, overworld map, and a full game loop under a real deadline.

HOW TO PLAY

Browser: click "Run game" above. Works best in Chrome on desktop. On phone, use the on-screen touch buttons.

Windows download: unzip, double-click PLAY.bat (needs Python 3.12 installed).

CONTROLS

Arrow keys — move
Space — jump
X — attack
Enter — enter level / confirm
P — pause

On mobile browser: use the on-screen D-pad and buttons.
